Hi all, my name is Tanya Holczer.
I am 37 years old (almost 38, and still in denial about it), married with 3 children.
This is Langley, British Columbia. My home for the past 8 months.
I've been playing World of Warcraft since 2009.
I enjoy reading books very much (I own over 3,000 books), riding horses, and my new passion - skiing.

This is my neighborhood, Willoughby. We've decided to move here so that my almost-10-year-old daughter, Alice, could swim in the Langley Olympians, the best swim team in western Canada.

These are my kids:
Alice, 10, a gifted swimmer (now competing for the Regional team);
Connor, 7, just started studying martial arts;
Lyra, 1.5, favorite pastime is eating.
The picture was taken during the first snow of the year. Unfortunately, we don't get enough of these in the region.
We moved here because we love cold weather, snow, and rain, and despise heat.
It's very challenging because I'm studying at college for a PBD in programming and IT full-time, and Alice and Connor have each 5 practices a week (Alice even has swim practice at 5:45 AM on Thursdays!😲), and Lyra is a little rascal.

This picture was taken in Israel.
These are our two dogs - Skye, a Border Collie, and Marshall, a Boxer. They are 4 years old.
We took them with us to Canada. Because no airline would fly Boxers (or any snub-nosed dogs) to Canada, we had to fly to LA and make a road trip by car to Vancouver. It took us approximately 1600km and 5 days to complete, and it was a challenge to load an SUV with all our luggage, two dogs, three kids, and two adults. Still can't believe we moved here with only six suitcases..
