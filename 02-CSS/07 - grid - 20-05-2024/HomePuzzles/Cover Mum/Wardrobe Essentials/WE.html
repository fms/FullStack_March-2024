Welcome to Cover Mum's Wardrobe Essentials!

From belts, scarves, and jewels to essential dresses, jeans and shoes, here you will find everything you need to build the perfect wardrobe that will get you through every season effortlessly. All items suggested are deliberately intended to be mixed and matched to create a versatile and complete wardrobe which will never let you down whatever the occasion - school run, work, evening, weekend casual...

I never discriminate between the high street and the higher end fashion (that is when I can afford the latter). There is great stuff to be found in both, and combined, they create amazing styles. Consistently, the brands represented below include the cheap as well as the more expensive.
